Reduce Salt Intake

Cutting back on salt helps lower blood pressure and reduces strain on your heart — a small change with big benefits.

🏃

Exercise Regularly

Daily movement improves insulin sensitivity and keeps your heart strong — even a brisk 30-minute walk works wonders.

🩸

Monitor Blood Sugar & BP

Regular tracking helps you and your doctor spot trends early and adjust treatment before complications arise.

🚭

Avoid Smoking & Alcohol

Both tobacco and excess alcohol spike blood pressure and worsen diabetes control — quitting is a game changer.

Consistency with prescribed medication keeps your numbers stable and prevents long-term damage.
